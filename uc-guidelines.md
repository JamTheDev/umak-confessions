# UMak Confessions General Community Guidelines  
**Revised: May 3, 2020**

## General Disclaimer and Indemnification
UMak Confessions is not a media entity but a confession files community and is not liable to any damages or defamation that may occur. Any confessions being posted—whether good or bad, in any way, shape, or form—will be solely the responsibility and liability of the person who confessed in our community.

UMak Confessions will remain neutral to any confessions being posted on our page, and it is on the netizens’ or viewers’ discretion whether to believe the content of a post within our community.

Likewise, this page will never reveal the identity of the person who confessed in our community for his/her protection and security and as a sign of respect to those who trusted our community to be their voice.

By sending your stories to us, we assume that you have read and understood the terms and conditions stated here.

---

## I. Format
Senders are requested to follow the suggested format:

```

Title
Confession
Fake Name
Yr/College

```

**Title**  
– Must be short and interesting  

**Confession**  
– The body of the story/entry itself  

**Fake Name**  
– A non-identifiable name of the sender. If left blank, the default will be **“Anonymous UMakian.”**  

**Year and College**  
– Suggested for audience recognition. May be left blank if the entry is sensitive and may harm the college.

---

## II. Entry Reference Number
Your entry will be given an entry number once approved and posted. This will serve as a reference for both UMak Confessions and the sender for retrieval and archival purposes.

---

## III. Post Category
Along with the entry number, UMak Confessions will categorize your confession as follows:

**a. #UMakConfessions**  
Contains love stories, love confessions, or naughty stories that may entertain or inspire audiences. Also includes stories about good moments or burdens from past relationships, bad companions, and family conflicts.

**b. #UMakDiaries**  
Stories shared by students about their daily lives on or off campus.

**c. #UMakiALAMka**  
Complaints, comments, and suggestions from students that need action from officials or addressed parties.  
All system-, official-, student council-, local organization-, or admin-related concerns must go to **UMak Online Action Center.**

**d. #UCX or #UMakWILDConfessions**  
Stories involving explicit content, including nudity, sex, obscenity, and lewd acts. These entries undergo thorough evaluation before posting.  
Post visibility is restricted to audiences **18 years old and above**.

**e. #AskUMakian**  
Questions, advice requests (life, love life, career, etc.), instructions, inquiries, requirements, processes, systems, and schedules (subject to evaluation).

**f. #UMakiSALITA**  
Spoken-word pieces and poetry from senders.

**g. #UMakPABATIWeekends**  
Greetings from UMakians to their crushes. Least prioritized due to repetitive nature.

---

## IV. Composition of the Story
- Avoid j3j3m0hwn texts  
- Avoid foul and obscene language  
- All entries are evaluated individually  
- Entries that violate guidelines will not be published  
- Be creative and concise; avoid unnecessarily long stories  
- Avoid mentioning real names unless the mention is positive or humorous

---

## V. Posting Your Story
- Do not demand immediate posting  
- All stories are screened before posting  
- Posting order does not guarantee recency  
- Senders may or may not be notified once posted due to anonymity

---

## VI. Technologies Used
UMak Confessions uses several platforms for receiving confessions, all subject to the same evaluation guidelines.

### GForms
Used by most related online communities. Provides structured and complete story submission.  
Allows anonymous submission without social media use.

Submission links:  
- theumakconfessions.com/send  
- bit.ly/umakconfessionsform

### E-mail
Send stories to: **umakconfessions@gmail.com**  
Stories must follow the general format.

### FB Page Inbox
Stories sent via Messenger are subject to evaluation.

### Harold Heron’s Messenger
Stories may be sent via personal message to **Harold Heron**, founder of UMak Confessions.  
All submissions undergo evaluation.

---

## VII. Evaluation

### Profanity and Obscenity
Any foul or offensive language may be removed, censored, or replaced.

### Allowed Senders
- Currently enrolled students  
- Senior High School students  
- College students  
- Post-graduate students  
- University of Makati professors and employees  
- Alumni  
- Outsiders  

**Prohibited Senders**
- Junior High School students  
- Elementary students  
- Children below 18 years old

---

## Data Collection and Privacy
Senders may optionally provide contact details.

UMak Confessions may collect anonymous demographic data for service improvement. All data is handled confidentially.

---

## Rejection of Entries
UMak Confessions reserves the right to reject entries with or without informing the sender. All senders are expected to review these guidelines before submission.

---

## Third-Party Sending
Entries submitted on behalf of another person will undergo strict evaluation.  
Common indicators include phrases like *“pinapasa lang po sa akin”* or *“sent through my account but not about me.”*  
Suspicious or unauthorized entries may be rejected.

---

## Rebuttal and Replies
If an entry mentions individuals or organizations and is found to be malicious, fake, or libelous, UMak Confessions may request rebuttals or clarifications.  
Responses may be posted as comments or as separate posts.

---

## School Guidelines Compliance
UMak Confessions abides by University of Makati guidelines, student handbooks, and official protocols.  
Entries violating these rules will be revoked.

---

## Coordination with Proper Authorities
UMak Confessions may coordinate with authorities if an entry:
- Contains libelous statements  
- Requires settlement among parties  
- Poses harm or danger  
- Threatens community, school, or national security  
- Disrupts official operations or protocols  

UMak Confessions reserves the right not to post such entries and to pursue legal remedies where applicable.

---

## Requests for Alteration or Removal
Appeals for modification or removal of entries will be evaluated.  
UMak Confessions may approve or deny requests at its discretion.

---

## News and Official Announcements
UMak Confessions may publish verified announcements from authorized offices or entities.  
Authorized personnel may contact UMak Confessions via email, FB page, or Harold’s Messenger.

Verification will be conducted before publication.

---

## Inquiries
School-related inquiries may receive automated responses.  
UMak Confessions may redirect inquiries to official pages such as **facebook.com/UMakPH**.

---

## Confidentiality
UMak Confessions will never disclose sender identities.  
Disclosure requests may be granted or denied at the discretion of UMak Confessions.

All final decisions of UMak Confessions are conclusive.

---

## Anti-Bullying / Libel Advocacy
UMak Confessions does not tolerate bullying or libelous content in compliance with **Republic Act No. 10627**.  
Entries violating this policy may be rejected without notice.

---

## Philippine Data Privacy Protection
All information provided is handled with confidentiality and in accordance with Philippine data privacy laws.

---

## Questions
Contact us via email: **umakconfessions@gmail.com**
